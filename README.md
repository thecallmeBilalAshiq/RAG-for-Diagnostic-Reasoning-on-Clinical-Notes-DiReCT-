# RAG-for-Diagnostic-Reasoning-on-Clinical-Notes-DiReCT-
Model: Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.2 (4-bit quantized) | Embedding: all-MiniLM-L6-v2 | Vector DB: Chroma

In the world of Natural Language Processing (NLP), the ability to process and understand human language is advancing rapidly. One of the key innovations that are paving the way for this evolution is the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) approach. The NLP — RAG Project aims to leverage this cutting-edge technology to improve information retrieval and text generation systems.

- Project Overview 🧠
Our project brings together talented minds to work on developing systems that can retrieve information and generate coherent, relevant answers. By combining retrieval techniques with language models, we aim to make the process of answering queries more accurate and reliable.

Our goal is to transform the way machines handle natural language by enhancing their ability to retrieve relevant information and generate contextually accurate answers. This combination is what makes RAG-based systems a powerful tool for the future of NLP.

- Key Highlights ✨
Improved Information Retrieval: By implementing advanced retrieval techniques, we ensure that our systems find the most relevant documents from a large dataset.
Contextual Text Generation: After retrieving the relevant documents, our system uses language models to generate coherent and precise responses based on the context.
Real-World Applications: This approach is crucial for domains such as healthcare, customer service, legal, and education where providing accurate and reliable information is critical.
